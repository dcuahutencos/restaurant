About Us

Armando's is a family-owned and operated restaurant, proudly run by three brothers who grew up learning the art of pizza-making from their father. Their dedication to the Italian cuisine is reflected in every dish we serve. Whether it's our handcrafted pizzas, house-made pastas, or desserts, every recipe carries the legacy of generations past.

We take pride in being a family-oriented restaurant, where friends, families, and food lovers gather to share a meal and create memories. From locals who grew up with us in Canarsie to new friends discovering us in Brooklyn, Armando's Pizza remains a place where everyone feels at home.

Join us for a taste of tradition, where every slice tells a story, and every dish is made with love. At Armando's, our family is always ready to welcome yours.
